Ryan in a nutshell

I am 27 years old and from San Diego, CA. Specifically, I am from Encinitas, a small beach town in Northern San Diego County. Growing up I spent most of my free time surfing and exploring San Diego with my friends. As I got older, I picked up some new hobbies, though, surfing is still my favorite of them. My new hobbies included traveling, playing music, and coding.

Currently, I live in Santa Barbara, CA. I resigned from my previous job in September '22 in favor of working at my favorite wine bar & tap house - Corks & Crowns - while I build a portfolio and train up on the skills I need to land my next job as a software developer. I still plan on working one day per week at Corks & Crowns after landing my next role though, being there feels like being at my home away from home.
